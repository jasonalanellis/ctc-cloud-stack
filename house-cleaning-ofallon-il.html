Common Mistakes in house cleaning ofallon il Homeowners Make

Many homeowners inadvertently undermine their cleaning efforts through common missteps: * Using the Wrong Chemistry: Applying an acidic cleaner to a surface that requires an alkaline solution (or vice-versa) can be ineffective or even damaging. For instance, using vinegar (acidic) on hard water stains (alkaline) will work, but using it on a delicate stone counter is a recipe for etching. * Insufficient Dwell Time: Disinfectants and specialized cleaners require specific contact times to work effectively. Wiping them off too quickly renders them useless against bacteria and viruses. * Cross-Contamination: Using the same cloth for the toilet and then the kitchen counter is a direct route to spreading germs. Professional cleaners adhere to strict color-coding protocols for cloths and tools. * Ignoring Indoor Air Quality: Neglecting HEPA filtration or regular HVAC filter changes allows dust and allergens to recirculate, making any surface cleaning effort temporary. * Over-Wetting Carpets: Excessive water during carpet cleaning can lead to mold growth and fiber damage. Proper extraction is key to leaving carpets clean and dry within hours.
