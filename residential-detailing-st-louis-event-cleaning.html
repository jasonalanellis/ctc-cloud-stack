Common Mistakes in Event Prep

Many homeowners make critical errors when trying to execute residential cleaning and detailing for St. Louis events on their own. The most common mistake is over-wetting hardwood floors. Excessive water causes wood fibers to swell and eventually cup, ruining the flat profile of your flooring. Always use a damp-mopping system with a highly wrung microfiber pad instead.

Another frequent error is using abrasive pads on stainless steel appliances. This creates micro-scratches that permanently ruin the factory grain. Always wipe parallel to the grain using a non-abrasive microfiber cloth and a dedicated surface tension reducer.

Finally, neglecting touchpoints is a major oversight. Light switches, door handles, and cabinet pulls accumulate sebum (skin oil) which dulls their appearance. These require targeted degreasing to restore their original luster before guests arrive.
