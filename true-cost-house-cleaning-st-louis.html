What is the true cost house cleaning st louis residents pay for a first-time service?

For a first-time service, homeowners should expect a minimum floor of $300. Why is there such a significant price difference between deep cleaning and standard cleaning?

Deep cleaning requires specialized techniques, stronger physical effort, and more time to break down heavy buildup. Standard cleaning is designed to maintain an already clean home, focusing on surface-level dust and basic upkeep. A deep clean often involves detailed hand-scrubbing of baseboards, light fixtures, and tile grout, which requires more labor-intensive processes.

Yes, larger homes and those with pets typically require more time and specialized equipment, which will affect the final flat-rate pricing. Pet hair requires extra vacuuming passes with HEPA-filter equipment to ensure complete extraction from carpets and upholstery. When requesting a quote, always be transparent about the size of your home and the presence of pets to ensure an accurate estimate.

How often should I schedule recurring cleaning services?

Most busy households in the St. Louis suburbs benefit most from bi-weekly recurring services.
